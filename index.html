🎳 Match Official Guide

1. Setup: Select your Game Format (Singles to Fours) and Match Ends. Enter player names from Lead up to Skip.

2. Scoring: Tap A Win or B Win to open the keypad. If the jack is burned, tap ⚠️ Record Dead End; this records the event without affecting the score or end count.

3. Editing: Made a mistake? Long-press (hold) any scored row in the table to re-open the keypad and correct the shots.

4. Signatures: Tap the signature boxes to open a Full Screen signing area. Ensure both Skips sign before sharing.

5. WhatsApp Share: Tapping Share Result creates a high-res image snapshot of the entire screen, including rosters and signatures.
