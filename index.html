How to use Pro Bowls

  • Scoring: Tap Team A or B to record shots. Keypad max shots adjust per format (e.g., max 8 for Pairs and Fours).
  • Dead Ends: Use the "Dead End" button to record a burnt end without changing the score.
  • Edit Scores: Long press (5 seconds) on any row in the history table to alter that end's score.
  • Signing: When the match finishes, tap the Skip signature boxes to open a full-screen signing area.
  • Share: Generate a high-quality image of the final scorecard to share via text or email.
